Welcome

Lynn Miller Nichols passed away on _____ after a 10 year battle with Alzheimer’s disease. Over the last several years, very few of our extended family and friends have seen Lynn or been aware of her illness. We decided to create this site as a way to help the people in Lynn’s life connect with her story and contribute their memories and photos of her.


We encourage you to share this website with anyone who knew and loved Lynn. It’s our hope that while it’s impossible for us to know everyone who touched Lynn’s life, this will eventually finds its way to them.
